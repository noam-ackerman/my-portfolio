About-Me

I'm Noam Ackerman

Over the past few years, I’ve gained extensive experience as a frontend developer, delivering data-driven features and creating engaging, responsive web applications. I’ve worked across various industries, contributing to projects ranging from e-commerce platforms to gaming and legal services.

My expertise includes developing clean, reusable code, integrating APIs, and designing and building intuitive user interfaces. I have significant experience working with technologies and tools such as React, Next.js, TypeScript, JavaScript, CSS, Tailwind, Git, and UI libraries including Radix, Shadcn, MUI, and more.

When I am not coding, I’m a musician (singer, songwriter, instrumentalist and music producer) and a visual artist currently active under the name “Noamle”.

I am spending a lot of time self-learning things I am passionate about driven by curiosity, will power, self-discipline and diligence.
I am a hard worker, fast learner and a motivated person who finds pleasure in facing challenges and solving problems.
A healthy, productive and respectful communication is a top value for me in any social or professional interaction.
