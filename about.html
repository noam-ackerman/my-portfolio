About-Me

I'm Noam Ackerman

I am from Israel and recently moved back after 5 years of living in Berlin. I’ve started my journey with coding at “SheCodes USA” courses. My studies involved learning and applying ReactJS, JSX, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, UI-UX Development, API integration, VS code, Git, Figma, Bootstrap, responsive design and more...

I started coding wanting to expend my horizon due to my deep passion for technology, design, arrangements and logical creative thinking.

In my previous job, I’ve been working in Berlin as a frontend developer at “Nosto Solutions” - an E-commerce experience platform. I’ve been creating and integrating dynamic, interactive and responsive interfaces for merchants. Taking ownership of features from Figma to production. I’ve learned a lot about investigating different environments, creating solutions and optimizing user experience, while leveling up my coding and developing skills. Currently I'm continuing to strengthen my React skills and also learning TypeScript and NodeJS.

When I am not coding, I’m a musician (singer, songwriter, instrumentalist and music producer) and a visual artist currently active under the name “Noamle”.

I am spending a lot of time self-learning things I am passionate about driven by curiosity, will power, self-discipline and diligence.
I am a hard worker, fast learner and a motivated person who finds pleasure in facing challenges and solving problems.
A healthy, productive and respectful communication is a top value for me in any social or professional interaction.
