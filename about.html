About-Me

I am Noam Ackerman

I'm 25 years old, from Israel and currently based in Berlin.
I’ve started my journey with coding at “SheCodes” courses.
My studies involved learning and applying HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, UI and web Development, API integration, bootstrap, VS codes, GitHub, responsive design and more…

I started coding wanting to expend my horizon due to my deep passion for technology, design, arrangements and logical creative thinking. When I am not coding, I’m a musician (singer, songwriter, instrumentalist and music producer) and a visual artist currently active under the name “Noamle”. I have also studied sound design and music production at “Catalyst Berlin” (formerly "dbs music").

I am spending a lot of time self-learning things I am passionate about driven by curiosity, will power, self-discipline and diligence.
I am a hard worker, fast learner and a motivated person who finds pleasure in facing challenges and solving problems.
A healthy, productive and respectful communication is a top value for me in any social or professional interactions.
