About-Me

I'm Noam Ackerman

I was born and raised in Israel and recently move back to my Homeland after 5 years of living in Berlin.
I’ve started my journey with coding at “SheCodes” courses. My studies involved learning and applying HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, JSX, UI and web Development, API integraYon, bootstrap, vs codes, GitHub, Figma, responsive design and more...

I started coding wanting to expend my horizon due to my deep passion for technology, design, arrangements and logical creative thinking.

In the Last 1.5 years, I’ve been working in Berlin as a frontend developer at “Nosto Solutions” - an E-commerce personalization platform. I’ve been creating dynamic and interactive features for costumers and integrating them on their sites. I’ve learned a lot about investigating different environments, creating solutions and optimizing user experience, while leveling up my coding and developing skills.Currently I'm continuing to strengthen my React skills and also learning NodeJS.

When I am not coding, I’m a musician (singer, songwriter, instrumentalist and music producer) and a visual artist currently active under the name “Noamle”. I have also studied sound design and music production at “Catalyst Berlin” (formerly "dbs music").

I am spending a lot of time self-learning things I am passionate about driven by curiosity, will power, self-discipline and diligence.
I am a hard worker, fast learner and a motivated person who finds pleasure in facing challenges and solving problems.
A healthy, productive and respectful communication is a top value for me in any social or professional interactions.
